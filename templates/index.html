üî¨ What Are Mersenne Primes?

Mersenne primes are prime numbers of the form 2^p - 1, where p is itself a prime number. They are among the rarest and most valuable numbers in mathematics.

Mersenne Prime Formula

// Mersenne Prime: 2^p - 1
// Example: 2^3 - 1 = 8 - 1 = 7 (Prime!)
// Example: 2^5 - 1 = 32 - 1 = 31 (Prime!)
// Example: 2^7 - 1 = 128 - 1 = 127 (Prime!)
